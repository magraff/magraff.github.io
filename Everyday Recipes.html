Turkey Chili

Ingredients

  1. 1 pound of ground turkey
  2. 2 cups of finely chopped white onions
  3. 2 tablespoons of minsed garlic
  4. 1 cup of quartered chopped celery
  5. 1 cup of finley chopped jalapeno
  6. 1 tablespoon of chopped oregano
  7. 2 whole bay leaves
  8. 3 cups of canned diced tomatoes
  9. 3 tablespoons of chili powder
  10. 2 teaspoons of ground cumin
  11. 2 cup sof chicken broth
  12. 15 ounce can of drained red kidney beans
  13. shredded cheese to taste
  14. salt and pepper to taste
  15. sour cream to taste for garnish
